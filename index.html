About Me

Hello! I’m Tyler Schrimper, a music producer and music engineer currently studying communications at UNC Chapel Hill. My passion lies in crafting immersive audio experiences and pairing them with compelling visuals. Whether I’m mixing tracks in the studio or illustrating an infographic in Adobe InDesign or Illustrator, I strive to tell stories that resonate. Below you’ll find examples of my design work as well as a sample of my music production.

Design Portfolio

Addiction Infographic

A two‑page spread examining the science behind social media addiction and the methods platforms use to keep users engaged. Designed using Adobe InDesign and Illustrator, this piece demonstrates my ability to organize complex information into a clear, compelling layout. Custom vector icons and typographic hierarchy guide the reader through the narrative.

Poison Dart Frogs

An illustrated research spread about the world’s most poisonous organisms. Combining hand‑drawn artwork, Photoshop compositing and Illustrator, this design showcases creative illustration techniques alongside disciplined information design. The result is an engaging educational piece with scientific accuracy.
